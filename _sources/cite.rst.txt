How to cite ARC
===============

Text form::

    A. Grinberg Dana, D. Ranasinghe, H. Wu, C. Grambow, X. Dong, M. Johnson, M. Goldman, M. Liu, W.H. Green,
    "ARC - Automated Rate Calculator", version 1.1.0, https://github.com/ReactionMechanismGenerator/ARC,
    DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.3356849


BibTeX form::

    @misc{ARC,
      author = {Grinberg Dana, A. and Ranasinghe, D. and Wu, H. and Grambow, C. and Dong, X. and Johnson, M. and Goldman, M. and Liu, M. and Green, W.H.},
      title = {ARC - Automated Rate Calculator, version 1.1.0},
      year = {2019},
      publisher = {GitHub},
      journal = {GitHub repository},
      howpublished = {\url{https://github.com/ReactionMechanismGenerator/ARC}},
      doi = {10.5281/zenodo.3356849}
    }
